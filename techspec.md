## 2. Information Architecture and Page Structure
The website is designed with a single-page application (SPA) structure or a linearly flowing long page, equipped with a navigation system to facilitate access to each section. The following is the website's information architecture structure:

### A. Navigation Bar (Header)
The navigation bar is located statically at the top of the page to ensure consistent accessibility for users while browsing the site. The navigation menu consists of:
- **Home:** Redirects to the top section (Hero Section).
- **All Work:** Directs to the catalog of completed projects.
- **About:** Directs to the detailed profile and background of the portfolio owner.
- **Resume:** A link or specific section to view the professional curriculum vitae.

### B. Main Section (Hero Section)
The first section seen by visitors upon opening the website. The elements contained within include:
- **Professional Name:** "Zakia Azharrifa Kn" displayed with dominant typography.
- **Professional Title:** "UI / UX DESIGNER" as an affirmation of career focus.
- **Brief Description:** A design vision statement that reads: *"I design interfaces that are not only visually appealing but also comfortable and easy to use. By combining user layout, modern aesthetics, and meaningful interactions, I help deliver interfaces that are memorable and comfortable to use."*
- **Profile Picture:** A visual element in the form of a circular self-portrait on the right side of the explanatory text.

### C. Specialization Section (What I Specialize In)
This section provides affirmation regarding the focus of services offered, divided into two main categories with supporting visual device icons (smartphone and laptop):
- **UI/UX Mobile Design:** Focuses on designing intuitive, visually appealing, and easy-to-use mobile application interfaces.
- **UI/UX Website Design:** Focuses on designing responsive website interfaces oriented towards user comfort.

### D. Featured Projects Catalog (Featured Projects)
Showcases a portfolio of actual completed work. Each project is displayed in a card format containing the title, project description, brief case study, design preview (mockup), and an interactive action button (**Demo Project**). The showcased projects include:
- **Cookie:** A mobile recipe application to help users create, discover, save, and follow cooking steps easily.
- **SCM Website:** Design of a Supply Chain Management website for industrial scale, ranging from dashboards, inventory, warehousing, to delivery monitoring.
- **Wireframe Santry Cyber:** Wireframe design of an online learning application accessible to all students in Indonesia.
- **Website Kamal Group:** A digital-based company profile providing photography studio, printing, and internship services.
- **Trains Ticket:** Redesign of the train ticket booking feature on the Traveloka application, focusing on the ease of seat selection and checkout process.
- **Gonyam:** An application to help culinary MSMEs (Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises) promote and sell food products digitally with a user-friendly interface.

### E. Profile Section (About)
An in-depth profile section explaining the owner's background and competencies:
- **Background:** A 6th-semester Information Systems student focusing on UI/UX Design.
- **Work Methodology:** Accustomed to being involved in the entire end-to-end design process, from user research, problem formulation, wireframing, prototyping, to usability testing.

## 3. Visual Design and Interaction Analysis
- **Color Scheme:** Utilizes a soft dominant color palette with a pastel pink and white gradient background, providing a modern, clean, creative, and feminine yet professional impression.
- **Typography:** Uses clean modern sans-serif fonts to ensure high readability on both desktop and mobile devices.
- **Interactive Elements:** The **Demo Project** button uses an active link that directs users straight to the external Figma platform page. This allows visitors or reviewers to explore the interactive prototype directly in the designer's native workspace.
